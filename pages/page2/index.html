Juggernaut

A medium carry, Juggernaut can support his teammates with Healing Ward, and escape dangerous situations with Blade Fury. In a one-on-one fight, Omnislash is the most powerful single target ability in the game, usually killing his opponent if no other enemies are around to absorb the damage.

Phantom Assassin

Phantom Assassin blurs her body, increasing her movement speed, causing her to be impossible to see until she attacks or comes near enemy heroes, towers, or outposts. Phantom Assassin refines her combat abilities, acquiring a chance of gaining Deadly Focus with each attack.

Learn More

Invoker

Invoker forges a spirit embodying the strength of fire and fortitude of ice. Damage, health, and armor are based on the level of Exort while attack range, mana, and duration are based on the level of Quas. The elemental's scorching attack is capable of melting the armor of enemy heroes.
